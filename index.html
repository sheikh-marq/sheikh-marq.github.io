About Sheikh Marq

I am Sheikh Marq, the professional creative identity of Shaik Musharaf Alam (widely known as Musharaf Smart). Based in Kuwait, I specialize in multidisciplinary visual creation, bridging the gap between artistic design and advanced technology.

Available for projects in Kuwait and internationally, bringing clarity and craft to every visual communication challenge.
