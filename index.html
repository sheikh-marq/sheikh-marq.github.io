About Sheikh Marq

Who is Sheikh Marq?

A premium creative brand built on one principle: every visual decision must have a reason.

🎓 Former Design Faculty & Educator

Sheikh Marq is the creative brand of Shaik Musharaf Alam — also known online as Musharaf Smart. A graphic designer and brand consultant based in Kuwait, originally from India.

Started as a design student and was recognised early for exceptional skills — later promoted to design faculty, conducting professional training sessions on design principles, software, and creative workflows. That foundation of teaching sharpened a rare ability: explaining the why behind every visual decision.

I specialise in building complete brand identity systems for companies who want to be permanently remembered — not temporarily noticed. I combine strategic thinking with premium visual execution across graphic design, AI-assisted workflows, digital marketing, and website design.

Based in Kuwait City, serving clients across the GCC and internationally. No agency overhead. Direct senior-level creative expertise. Every project receives my full attention.
